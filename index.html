About Ceylon Cinnamon

Known as the “True Cinnamon,” Ceylon cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum) is prized worldwide for its delicate flavor, sweet aroma, and remarkable health benefits. Unlike common cassia cinnamon, it’s softer, lighter in color, and contains lower coumarin levels, making it safer for regular use.
